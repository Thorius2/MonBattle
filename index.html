MonBattles

Welcome to the most low-cost and worst balanced pokemon game in the entire web. I can't promise you'll enjoy it, but hey, at least it's free!

How to Play:

1) Choose a number between 1 and 898, and type it in the field "PokeNumber".

2) Choose the stat that you want to use against your oponent. The one with the highest stat wins!

3) Click on the "Fight" button to start. The computer will then select a random Pokemon. The one with the higher stat, will be the winner and earn a point.

4) Once either you or the computer win 6 points, the match will end. Keep in mind that you can't use the same Pokemon in a single round.

5) In case of tie, the player will earn the point (the game is bad but the dev is good :D)
